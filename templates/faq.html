Getting Started

Getting started is easy! Simply register for an account, log in, and upload your resume. Our AI-powered system will automatically generate a professional portfolio based on your resume content.

We currently support PDF and DOCX file formats for resume uploads. These formats ensure the best parsing accuracy for our AI enhancement system.

AI Enhancement Features

Our AI system analyzes your resume content and enhances the descriptions of your experiences using advanced natural language processing. It maintains the authenticity of your achievements while making them more impactful and professional.

Yes! While our AI provides initial enhancements, you have full control to edit and customize the content to better match your preferences and professional voice.

Portfolio Customization

We offer a variety of professional themes designed for different industries and preferences. Each theme is fully responsive and optimized for both desktop and mobile viewing.

Once your portfolio is ready, you can easily share it using a unique URL. You can control the privacy settings to make your portfolio public or private as needed.

Account & Security

We take data security seriously. Your resume and portfolio data are encrypted and stored securely. We never share your information with third parties without your explicit consent.

You can manage your account settings, including password changes and privacy preferences, through your dashboard. Look for the settings icon after logging in.
